About the Architect

Michael Archie

Founder & Chief Ecosystem Architect

I build the infrastructure ecosystems that AI factories and HPC data centers need to actually get built.

Michael Archie is a strategic infrastructure architect and ecosystem builder operating at the intersection of AI data centers, critical power systems, and federal technology procurement. Based in Coral Gables, Florida, he founded MKA Critical Power to solve a problem no one else was addressing: the massive gap between emerging infrastructure technologies and the integrated, bankable solutions that enterprise and government buyers actually need.

With a background spanning business development, federal contracting, and energy infrastructure, Michael brings a rare combination of technical fluency and deal-making expertise. He has spent his career connecting the dots between power generation, cooling systems, modular compute, and clean energy — assembling them into deployable ecosystems that can withstand federal scrutiny and attract institutional capital.

His approach is built on direct relationships with OEMs, national laboratory leaders, defense primes, and PE/VC investors. Michael doesn't just broker introductions — he architects the entire ecosystem from technology selection through consortium formation to deal closure.
